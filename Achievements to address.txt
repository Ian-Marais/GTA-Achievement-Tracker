https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Fq4lQl83atY

"HOW TO UNLOCK IT"
Complete one Heist Setup mission (a mission that is done to prepare for a final heist mission with pay out, Setup missions do not have payouts)

1: Requires rank 12
2: Requires high-end apartment with heist planning rooms for hosting which becomes availabe after Lester contacts you, map icon is a green H icon.
3: GTA Online only (not Story Mode)

Tips:
1: Co-op achievement (usually very early unlock).
2: 1. Do “The Fleeca Job” first it’s the easiest and shortest heist it only requires 2 players.
3: 2. You don’t need to host you can join another player’s setup.

Online

Pimp My Sidearm:
don't replace URL

"HOW TO UNLOCK IT"
1: Go to Ammu-Nation
2: Pick any weapon
3: Buy every available attachment/mod for it

Limitations:
You must unlock attachments first
Some mods are locked behind:
Story progress (Story Mode)
Rank level (Online)
Must buy ALL categories, missing even one category (e.g. tint or suppressor) = no unlock

Tips:
1. Use a cheap weapon
A basic pistol or sawed-off shotgun is cheapest to fully mod.
Some weapons only have 1–2 upgrades → easier unlock.

2. Free weapon trick (easy mode)
If you have Rockstar Social Club rewards, you can get a free Assault SMG
All mods can also be free.

Mode:
Story & Online
